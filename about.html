ABOUT

Welcome to Putit Itim, a vibrant community where young Filipino poets and writers come together to craft exceptional poems and explore the beauty of language. Founded on the shared passion for storytelling and poetic expression, Putit Itim celebrates the richness of Filipino culture through verse. Our community fosters collaboration and creativity, providing a supportive environment for writers to hone their craft and showcase their talents. From evocative narratives to poignant reflections, each piece penned by our members captures the essence of Filipino identity and resonates with profound emotion. Join us at Putit Itim as we continue to weave words into art, enriching the literary landscape with our unique perspectives and heartfelt expressions. Together, we uphold the power of poetry to inspire, provoke, and unite.
